## 一般疑问句的提问方式一(be动词)？
```
Are you...?   （经常的状态）
Is she...?
Am I...?           + 非动词
Were you...?  （过去（经常）的状态）
Was she...?
```
```
Are you at (home)?      prep.介词
Is she a (teacher)?     n.名词
Am I (right)?           adj.形容词
Were you (there?        adv.副词
Was she (your friend)?  pron.代词
```
```
练习：你是老师吗？ Are you a teacher?
你昨天在家吗？ Were you at home yesterday?
她上周缺课了吗？ Was she absent last week?
他刚刚在这吗？ Was he here just now?
她在北京吗？ Is she in Beijing?
```
## 一般疑问句的提问方式二（助动词）？
```
Are you...?   （此时此刻正在）
Is she...?
Am I...?           + doing...
Were you...?  （那是那刻正在）
Was she...?
```
```
Are you (studing English)?
Is she (cooking)?
Is he (having a bath)?
Were you (doing your homework)?
Was she (waiting for him)?
```
```
练习：你正在学法语吗？ Are you studying French?
你昨晚8点正在约会吗？ Were you having a date at 8:00 last night?
她正在度假吗？ Is she having a haliday?
我到的时候他正在读书吗？ Was he reading when I arrived?
```
## 一般疑问句的提问方式三（助动词）？
```
Are you...?   （现在的计划&打算）（客观、马上、计划）
Is she...?
Am I...?           + going to do...
Were you...?  （过去的计划&打算）
Was she...?
```
```
Are you going to study English?
Is she going to cook?
Is he going to have a bath?
Were you going to do your homework?
Was she going to wait for him?
```
```
练习：你打算学法语吗？ Are you going to study Frensh?
你晚上要约会吗？ Are you going to have a date tonight?
她以前打算过度假吗？ Was she going to have a holiday?
你们以前打算过出国读书吗？ Were you going to study abroad?
```
## 一般疑问句的提问方式四（助动词）？
```
Will you...?    (将要....)
Will she...?    + do(动词原形)
Shall I...?
Shall we...?
```
```
Will you study English?
Will she cook?
Will he have a bath?
Shall we have a holiday next month?
Shall I wait for him?
```
```
练习：你要学法语吗？ Will you study French?
他们晚上要约会吗？ Will they have a data tonight?
我们去度假好吗？ Shall we have a holiday?
你们出国读书吗？ Will you study abroad?
```
## 一般疑问句的提问方式五（助动词）？
```
Have you...?  (截止到现在已经完成)
Has she...?   + done(动词过去分词)
Had you...?
Had she...?  （截止过去已经完成）
```
```

Had she cooked the meal?
Had he had a bath?
Had you done your homework before 2:00?
Had she opened the door before I arrived?
```
```
练习：你学过法语吗？ Have you studied French?

你们度假前考试了吗？ Had you taken the exam before holiday?
你到之前火车走了吗？ Had the train left before you arrived?s
```
## 一般疑问句的提问方式六（助动词）？
```
Do you...?  （除了三单外用Do来提问，三单Dose提问）(习惯的动作)
Does she...?   + do（动词原形）
Did you\she...?  （过去（习惯的）动作）（不分人称）
```
```
Do you study English?
Does she cook?
Did she have a bath?
Does she do her homework?
Did you wait for him?
```
```
练习：你经常游泳吗？ Do you usually swim?
他经常约会吗？ Does he usually have a date?
你们去年度假了吗？ Did you have a holiday last year?
他今早赶上火车了吗？ Did he catch the train this morning?
```
## 一般疑问句的提问方式七（情态动词）？
```
Can you...?    (能够&可以)
Can she...?
Must I...?     （必须）+ do(动词原形)
May I...?      (能够&可以)（比can客气委婉）
Could（can的过去式） you...? （比can客气委婉）
```
```
Can you cook?
Can she swin?
May I have a seat?
Must I do that?
Could you show me another one?
```
```
练习：我可以请你看电影吗？ May I invite you to watch a movie?
你可以帮我一个忙吗？ Could you give me a hand?
你可以自己穿衣服吗？ Can you put on your coat by yourself?
我必须站在这吗？ Must I stand here?
```

## 特殊疑问句
### 1、含有be动词的提问：特殊疑问句+be+n.
```
你的名字是什么？ What is your name?
你为什么迟到？ Why were you late?
你什么时候在北京？When are you in Beijing?
```
### 2、含有助动词的提问：特殊疑问句+助动词...+n.
```
谁会帮你呢？ Who will help you?
你都去过那里呢？ Where have you been?
他正在读什么？ What is he reading?
```
### 3、含有情态动词的提问：特殊疑问句+情态动词+n.+v
```
我该做什么呢？ What must I do?
你几点能到？ When (What time) can you come?
他该如何做呢？ How must he do?
```
### 4、what\whose\which + n + be\助动词\情态动词... +n.
```
你喜欢什么颜色？ What color do you like?
这是谁的外套？ Whose coat is it?
你可以拼写哪些单词？Which(words) can you spell?
```
## 特殊疑问句练习：
```
你是做（什么）工作的？ What is your job?
你上周（为什么）那么忙？ Why were you so busy last week?
你（何时）在家？ When will you stay at home?
你以前爱（谁）啊？ Who(Whom Who单宾语时可以用whom) did you love?
（谁）昨天邀请你了啊？
这是（谁的）电影？ Whose movie is it?
你打算看（哪一部）电影？ Whose movie are you going to watch?
```
